Laserer

Ability: 4 Lasers: Shoot 4 lasers over 2 seconds: [EPS,Bow:15]
. -First Beam makes enemies take more knockback from 4th beam.
. -Second Beam makes enemies take 20% more damage from all sources.
. -Third Beam inflicts slowness I.
. -Last Beam inflicts 8 damage.

Kit: Diamond Helmet(Projectile Protection III) / Bow(Power I) / 2 Potions / 3 steak

Passive 1: Lasering Arrows: Whenever you land arrows on enemies, your arrow speed increases by 10%, up to 30%.
. -Arrow speed resets when your arrow fails to hit enemies.

Passive 2: Reversal: When you are below 35% HP, gain Resistance III and Speed II for 4 seconds.
. -Also, your melee hits give 10 energy during the duration. (cooldown: 40s)

Ultimate: Sniper Laser: Instantly shoot 1 laser that has all effects of the previous lasers!
